
NUTRITION PLAN - SAMPLE

DAILY CALORIE TARGET: 2000 calories

BREAKFAST (7:00 AM - 8:00 AM):
- 1 cup oatmeal with 1/2 cup berries
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 banana
Total: 350 calories

LUNCH (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM):
- 1 cup brown rice
- 4 oz grilled chicken breast
- 1 cup steamed broccoli
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 medium apple
Total: 450 calories

SNACK (3:00 PM):
- 1/4 cup mixed nuts
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
Total: 200 calories

DINNER (7:00 PM - 8:00 PM):
- 1 cup quinoa
- 4 oz salmon
- 1 cup mixed vegetables
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 small sweet potato
Total: 500 calories

EVENING SNACK (9:00 PM):
- 1 cup herbal tea
- 1 small piece of dark chocolate
Total: 50 calories

DRINKS:
- 8-10 glasses of water daily
- Green tea (2-3 cups)
- Avoid sugary drinks and alcohol

FOODS TO AVOID:
- Processed foods
- Added sugars
- Fried foods
- Excessive salt
- Alcohol

ALLOWED FOODS:
- Lean proteins (chicken, fish, tofu)
- Whole grains (brown rice, quinoa, oats)
- Fresh fruits and vegetables
- Healthy fats (olive oil, nuts, avocado)
- Low-fat dairy products

MEAL TIMING:
- Eat every 3-4 hours
- Don't skip meals
- Finish dinner 2-3 hours before bedtime
- Stay hydrated throughout the day

EXERCISE RECOMMENDATIONS:
- 30 minutes of cardio 5 days per week
- Strength training 3 days per week
- Stay active throughout the day

This plan is designed for weight maintenance and overall health improvement.
